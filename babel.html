Babel Artists' Pencils

Type:
Branding & Packaging
Year:
2023

This project involved designing a fictional identity and packaging according to a provided brief. This particular brief was for a range of artist pencils for which I designed the brand - Babel, that manufactures an artist pencils set consisting of coloured, charcoal and graphite pencils in one set.

Brand Name Significance

Babel is a reference to the well-known story of the tower of Babel. The city's people decided to build a tower to heaven to make a name for themselves. God saw this as arrogance and gave the singular-language speaking people different tongues thereby confusing the construction process. This story is referenced in the poem, "Ode (We are the Music Makers)", by Arthur O'Shaughnessey. This poem is about the transformative power of art that lies in the hands of artists; they have the power to bring about necessary change in the world. In the poem, it is mentioned that it is because of the genius of the artists that the humans could try and build the Tower of Babel. Though the story of Babel is often recited in the context of human pride, the poem refers to it as proof of the power of art. This is what the brand stands for, to realise the power of art and appreciate the artists and their work.

Packaging

The packaging comprises two components: the outer label and the box.

Label Design:

The label displays essential brand and product information, including price and barcode. It serves as the initial brand-customer interaction point, reflecting the brand's personality and values. Golden-yellow elements are gold foiled onto the label's surface, enhancing the brand's visual language.
